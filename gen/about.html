WImpiBLAST: A webUI for mpiBLAST

BLAST is the most extensively used sequence analysis program for sequence similarity search in large database of sequences. mpiBLAST program was developed as the parallelised program of BLAST to distribute the BLAST job accross many nodes and many processors of HPC Cluster to get the results faster.

We at NABI Computational Biology Lab have developed WImpiBLAST, a web GUI developed specifically for mpiBLAST. It is developed to give a user-friendly interface for biologist to submit BLAST job on HPC cluster.

Routinely most of the biologist use NCBI portal for doing sequence similarity search for their gene of interest. With the advent of Next Generation Sequencing Technologies it has now become possible to study gene expression at Genome wide scale through RNA-seq experiments. Annotation of all the genes in a given transcriptome is computationally very intensive job which can be accelerated by using High Performance Computing cluster.

To do this transcriptome annotation themselves, Biologist are often limited due to steep learning curve to gain skills of programming. WImpiBLAST will help biologist to overcome this limitation by helping them use high performance computing cluster for computationally intensive annotation jobs through a simple web interface.
